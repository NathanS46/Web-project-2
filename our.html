We are a

Sustainable Company

We know that producing food impacts the planet. We aim to reduce our impact and become the most sustainable company in all of the world.

We’re on a journey to become the most sustainable protein company – not just in Canada – but on earth. We are one of the only food companies in Canada to set science-based targets. We are carbon neutral. We aim to reduce our environmental footprint.
